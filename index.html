About Us

LecceTech Community is a non-profit tech community dedicated to promote technology, organise tech events/meetups and helping people learn the Technology. We are based in Lecce, Italy. We help people of all skill levels–from beginners to experts–discover what's possible. Bring yourself, your laptop (if possible) and a desire to learn and network at our next event!

FAQs

What do I need to bring?
Bring yourself, your laptop (if possible), a desire to learn, and all your friendship!

Who is running the LecceTech community?
Narain Sagar (@narainsagar), Giuseppe Silletti (@peppesilletti) and Camilla Minutilli (@ziaboby) runs the LecceTech community. Feel free to reach out for any questions OR you can also file an issue at our GitHub repo and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

What actually happens at LecceTech Community event?
LecceTech Community events/meetups are an opportunity to hang out with other learners and volunteers in a low stress, encouraging environment. Our typical event follows this schedule:
  • Doors open
  • Introduction & opening announcements (10-15 minutes)
  • Learning/mentoring (depending on event's type and timeline)
  • Networking and Feedback
  • Event ends

What is a Focused Session?
Focused Sessions happen during our LecceTech community events/meetups. While our general event is free-form and self-directed, our Focused Sessions are more of a lecture-style format led by a mentor on special topics. Keep an eye on our website and Facebook page for announcements!

How much does it cost to attend?
LecceTech Community is free for all! non-profit and open-source too!

How do I become a mentor?
We're always looking for people to volunteer in our community meetups! We ask that prospective volunteers:
  • Arrive atleast 20 minutes early at the event so we can get setup everything and have our mentor meeting.
  • Like our Facebook page and group for our future event updates
  • Join our discord channel to keep in touch with community members

Got any questions?
File an issue at our GitHub repo and we'll answer as soon as possible.

Code Of Conduct

LecceTech Community is dedicated to providing a harassment-free event experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of event participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any event venue, including talks, workshops, parties, Twitter and other online media. Event participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the event at the discretion of the event organisers.
